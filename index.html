Get Trained to Save Lives

Our QPR Gatekeeper training is designed for everyone, providing the skills and confidence to help someone in crisis.

For Individuals

Learn to recognize warning signs, ask the crucial questions, and persuade someone to get help.

For Education

Equip students, teachers, and staff in schools and colleges to create a supportive, aware community.

For the Workplace

Integrate mental health support into your company culture and empower employees to look out for one another.

For Professionals

Advanced courses for healthcare and mental health professionals to specialize in suicide intervention.
