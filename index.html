Our Mission & Impact

SPIF is one of India’s first organizations dedicated to preventing suicide. Partnering with the QPR Institute, a world leader in suicide prevention, our initiatives include raising public awareness, educating communities, and equipping individuals with the right tools to save lives.

Who Can Get Trained?

Our QPR Gatekeeper training is designed for everyone, providing the skills and confidence to help someone in crisis.

person

Learn to recognize warning signs, ask the crucial questions, and persuade someone to get help.
